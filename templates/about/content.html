About Us

We are a small family-run business specializing in restoring and selling vintage bikes. Every bike we sell tells a story and gets a new life, thanks to our passion and craftsmanship.

Our Story

Based in Slovakia, our family workshop is where passion meets precision. My dad, an avid bike enthusiast and craftsman, has been restoring bicycles for years. Together, we breathe new life into timeless brands like Colnago, Hetchins, Bianchi, and Peugeot. Every vintage bike we restore combines history, artistry, and functionality. We take pride in preserving cycling heritage and sharing it with enthusiasts worldwide.
